Welcome to DIVE
Manage your DIVE collections using the dashboard

 Manage my collections

Use the dashboard to upload new collections and/or curate your existing collections

When uploading new collections the system will verify if your dataset is DIVE compatible. We encourage you to get into contact with us, so we can help you get your collection into the right format.
When you already have a collection available in the system, it is possible to review annotations and/or manually edit incorrect or inappropriate data yourself.

Using the CrowdTruth DIVE offers collection owners to have their collections annotated either by crowd users or one or more different automatic text analysis tools.

 Enrich my collections

Enrich your collections using CrowdTruth's automated or crowd annotation functionalities

 Align my collections

Aligned your collections to similar/relevant collections that are already present in DIVE

DIVE offers the possibility to align your collection by using of our supported vocabularies, such as the GTAA.
